Carrot Sugar Cookie

ingredients

  • 1 cup, 2 sticks, unsalted butter (room temperature)
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 medium carrots (1/4 cup grated)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon powder
  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
